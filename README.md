**README**

**Problem Statement**
The objective of this project is to analyze a road accident dataset to identify patterns and factors that influence accident occurrence and severity. The goal is to explore the data using Power BI and derive meaningful insights that can help understand when and under what conditions accidents occur more frequently.

**Data Description**
A road accident dataset was used which contains multiple records of accidents along with attributes such as accident date and time, location details, severity level, weather conditions, road conditions, and visibility. The dataset includes a large number of accident entries that allow analysis of accident frequency across different time periods and environmental conditions. Before analysis, the dataset was cleaned by correcting data types, removing duplicates, replacing incorrect values, and organizing columns to make the data suitable for visualization and analysis.

**Key Insights**

* Accident frequency varies across different times of the day and days of the week.
* Weather conditions can influence accident occurrence and severity.
* Certain locations may show higher accident concentration compared to others.

**Recommendations**

* Improve safety measures and monitoring in high-risk areas.
* Increase driver awareness during high-risk time periods and adverse weather conditions.
* Use data-driven insights to support road safety planning and management.

**Tools Used**

* Power BI for data cleaning, transformation, and dashboard creation
* Excel for handling and exporting the dataset.
